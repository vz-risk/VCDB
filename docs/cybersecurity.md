# Cybersecurity Incident Response Plan

## Overview
This document outlines the steps to be taken in case of a cybersecurity incident, including ransomware attacks.

## Steps to Take
1. **Activate Incident Response Protocols**: Immediately activate the firm's incident response team upon detection of any security breach.
2. **Contain the Threat**: Implement containment measures to prevent further data exfiltration or corruption.
3. **Investigate the Incident**: Conduct a thorough investigation with the support of third-party cybersecurity professionals to identify the source and extent of the attack.
4. **Notify Law Enforcement**: Report the incident to relevant law enforcement agencies to assist in the investigation.
5. **Communicate with Affected Parties**: Inform clients and stakeholders about the incident, its impact, and any actions being taken.
6. **Review and Improve**: After the incident is resolved, review the response plan and make necessary improvements to enhance future preparedness.
